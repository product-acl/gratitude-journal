Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

Privacy First: Your gratitude journal entries are private and stored only on your device. We never read, access, or transmit your journal content to any servers.

1. Introduction

Welcome to 30-Second Gratitude Journal ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our mobile application.

By using our app, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Data You Provide

Journal Entries:

  • Your gratitude entries are stored locally on your device only
  • We never access, read, transmit, or store your journal content on our servers
  • Your entries remain completely private to you

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

Anonymous Analytics:

  • App usage: Screen views, feature usage, session duration
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Crash reports: Technical errors and app crashes (no personal data included)
  • Event tracking: Anonymous interactions like "entry created" or "export used" (content is never logged)

Purchase Information:

  • Transaction IDs and purchase status (managed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store)
  • We do not store your payment information
  • All payments are processed securely through Apple or Google's payment systems

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Analytics Data

We use anonymous analytics data to:

  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Understand which features are most valuable
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Optimize conversion rates and purchase flows

3.2 Purchase Data

We use purchase information to:

  • Validate your lifetime access purchase
  • Restore purchases on new devices
  • Provide customer support

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

4.1 Firebase Analytics (Google)

  • Purpose: Collect anonymous app usage data
  • Data collected: Device info, screen views, user interactions
  • Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4.2 Apple App Store / Google Play Store

  • Purpose: Process in-app purchases
  • Data collected: Transaction details, payment information
  • Privacy: Your payment information is handled directly by Apple or Google

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage

  • All journal entries are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage (AsyncStorage)
  • We do not have access to your device or your entries
  • If you uninstall the app, your local data will be deleted

5.2 Security Measures

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated
  • We never share individual user data with third parties

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Data Export

  • You can export your journal entries at any time as a CSV file
  • This allows you to keep a backup of your data

6.2 Data Deletion

  • You can delete individual entries from within the app
  • You can delete all your data using the "Delete All Data" feature in Settings
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data

6.3 Opt-Out of Analytics

Currently, analytics are automatically collected to improve the app. If you wish to opt out of analytics tracking, please contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy
